Bahasa Inggris

Pentingsari village is a tourist destination visited by countless people from different cities and even countries. This village is known for its rich traditions, beautiful natural scenery, and the engaging activities of Javanese life.

Opened to the public in 2008, Pentingsari offers activities like playing mud ball (bola lumpur), catching catfish with bare hands, and learning traditional dance. Visitors can also enjoy batik-making, playing gamelan instruments, and crafting grass puppets that test patience and creativity.

The peaceful atmosphere of the village—filled with the scent of rain and surrounded by rice fields, bamboo forests, scenic hills, and waterfalls—makes it the perfect escape from traffic and pollution.

The locals warmly welcome guests. My foster parents treated me and my friends like family. Our foster mother even made us tea every day without us asking. One memorable night, we were startled by a huge spider and caused a commotion. Instead of getting upset, our foster mom offered to help us, showing her incredible kindness and patience.
